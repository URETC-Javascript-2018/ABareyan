Welcome to Doug's Record Shop

About Us

Doug's is a record store in the center of Salt Lake City's downtown district. Doug started out as a vendor at swap meets and quickly grew a collection of vintage vinyl albums and CD's. Doug moved to the downtown location in 1990 and has been specializing in hard to find albums in rock, country, and classical music ever since. Drop on by and see what makes Doug's different!
